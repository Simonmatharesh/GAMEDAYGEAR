Our Story

Welcome to GameDayGear, a passion project turned reality by Simon Mathew. My journey with football jerseys began when I was just 12 years old, captivated by the rich history and stories woven into each design. What started as a young boy's fascination evolved into a serious collection when I turned 18, marking the beginning of a lifelong dedication to preserving and sharing football's heritage.

Growing up in India, I recognized the challenge many football enthusiasts faced: accessing authentic retro jerseys. This realization sparked the idea for GameDayGear. Our mission is simple yet powerful - to bridge this gap and make genuine retro football jerseys accessible to Indian football fans, ensuring that everyone can own a piece of football history.

Our Mission

To provide authentic retro football jerseys to Indian football enthusiasts, preserving the heritage of the beautiful game while making it accessible to everyone who shares our passion.

Authenticity

We guarantee the authenticity of every jersey in our collection, ensuring you receive genuine pieces of football history.

Accessibility

Making retro football jerseys accessible to Indian fans through competitive pricing and reliable delivery.

Community

Building a community of football enthusiasts who share our passion for the sport's rich heritage and iconic moments.
